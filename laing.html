Laing recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup bamboo shoots, sliced (1 used the vacuum packed ones)
  • 1 cup saluyot (jute leaves)
  • 1 pack spinach leaves or any leafy vegetables
  • 1 bunch string beans, sliced
  • 10 pcs okra, sliced in half
  • 2 tbsp bagoong monamon or ground anchovies in oil
  • fish sauce or sea salt, 1 red onion, chopped, 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups rice wash, fried fish, freshly ground black pepper, oil

Steps

  1. In a pot add oil then saut√© garlic and onions. Add okra and string beans then cook for 3 minutes. Add spinach and jute leaves and season with fish sauce and pepper then simmer for additional 2 minutes. Place vegetables on a bowl then top it with fried fish then serve. You can optionally add the fried fish on step 4 to make the soup richer but I prefer a crispy fried fish on the top.
  2. Dilute bagoong in rice wash water then pour into a strainer or muslin cloth over to the pot, press to extract all liquid. Bring to a boil.
  3. Add bamboo shoots then simmer for 5 minutes.
